Private QuickBooks Online Integration: Privacy Policy and Terms of Use

Last updated: June 21, 2026

App Purpose

This application is a private, non-public QuickBooks Online integration used only by the app owner for personal or internal accounting automation. It is not offered to third parties, is not listed on the QuickBooks App Store, and does not process data for external customers.

Privacy Policy

Data Accessed

The application may access QuickBooks Online accounting data that the app owner explicitly authorizes, such as customers, vendors, accounts, invoices, bills, payments, reports, or related accounting records, depending on the workflow being used.

Data Use

QuickBooks Online data is used only to support the app owner's personal or internal accounting workflows. The application does not sell, rent, share, or use QuickBooks Online data for advertising or marketing.

Data Storage and Retention

The application may store OAuth tokens, configuration settings, and limited operational logs needed to operate and troubleshoot the integration. QuickBooks Online data is retained only as needed for the stated accounting workflow.

Security

The application uses OAuth 2.0 for QuickBooks Online authorization. Client secrets, access tokens, and refresh tokens are protected using reasonable administrative, technical, and access-control safeguards. Production credentials and tokens are not published in source code or public repositories.

Data Sharing

QuickBooks Online data accessed by this application is not shared with third parties except as required to operate the app owner's authorized accounting workflow or as required by law.

User Control and Disconnection

The app owner may disconnect the application from QuickBooks Online at any time through QuickBooks Online app settings or by revoking the application's stored OAuth authorization.

Terms of Use

Private Use Only

This application is provided solely for the app owner's personal or internal use. It is not a public commercial software product and is not intended for use by third-party customers.

No Professional Advice

The application may assist with accounting automation, but it does not replace professional accounting, tax, legal, or financial review. The app owner remains responsible for reviewing all accounting outputs.

No Warranty

The application is provided as is, without warranties of any kind. Use of the application is at the app owner's discretion and risk.

Changes

This privacy policy and terms of use may be updated from time to time as the application changes.
